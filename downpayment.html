Down Payment

Which of you intending to build a tower sitteth not down first and Counteth the Cost?

Save for a
Down Payment

How much should you be saving?

Experts recommend at least 10% to 20% down. You will avoid the private mortgage insurance (PMI). This is an extra cost that is added to your monthly mortgage payment. This protects the lender because you didn't put enough down. If you forclose they benefit and are protected from greater loss.


Closing costs and prepaids:

Save another 2-3% of a home’s purchase price for closing costs and prepaids. Closing costs are the fees charged by title companies and lenders. While the prepaids cover any prorated property taxes and insurance items.



Moving and other expenses:

Where you are moving to can be very expensive too. Call moving companies for quotes ahead of time. Also if you plan to make changes like repainting, blinds, new furniture etc you’ll need money for that as well!



Pay off debt:

Try and save up three to six months of expenses in a savings account at the bank. You're the landlord now. And you'll be responsible for your own repairs. If you have money saved, then an unexpected repair won't rock your world. Are you ready financially? Counting the cost is easy when you have a plan.




Find the Right
Real Estate Agent


Do a lot of research on your own.

But you need an expert when it comes to actually finding and securing your home. A buyer’s agent can help you tremendously. They can help you find a house before it hits the market. And your agent will negotiate on your behalf so you don’t pay a penny more than the home is worth.

How much should you pay a buyer’s agent?

How does nothing sound? In most cases, the seller pays your real estate agent’s fees. Using a buyer’s agent is free to you. So have a true pro in your corner. Not all agents are created equal. Don’t work with an amateur. You want experience!
Buyer’s agent requirements:


  1. Full-time real estate experience for at least two years
  2. Great communication skills
  3. Makes you feel like you’re their only client
  4. Long list of sold homes every year
  5. Exceptional experience in your local market
